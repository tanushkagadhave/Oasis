Biography

Steven Paul "Steve" Jobs was an American information technology entrepreneur and inventor. He was the co-founder, chairman, and CEO of Apple Inc. Jobs was widely recognized as a pioneer of the microcomputer revolution of the 1970s and 1980s along with Apple co-founder Steve Wozniak.

Jobs also served as the CEO and majority shareholder of Pixar Animation Studios. His passion for innovation revolutionized industries including personal computers, smartphones, music, and digital publishing.
